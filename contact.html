About 4Front

4Front Technologies is a software development company with a focus on developing digital audio solutions for UNIX. Its founding partners are: Dev Mazumdar, Hannu Savolainen and George Yohng.

4Front's mission is to make Open Sound System a "de-facto"  audio API for UNIX. 4Front is a privately held company founded in 1992 with development centres in Finland and USA.
